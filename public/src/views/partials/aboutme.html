About Me

I'm Brian. I am a software developer living in San Francisco. I am originally from Austin Texas and I arrived here after working software roles at Accenture. When I moved I brought my love for technology, photography, art, and the outdoors with me.

My passion for programming started in late 2012, in the form of a simple web crawler. The goal of my project was to find all of the live music shows in the area at the time and match artists to my iTunes library. Although it was a small scripting project, it was the first time I fully immersed myself in the "coding world". Since those days of using Python to find my favorite artists, I turned my hobby into a career.

Technology

I am deeply interested in technology, design and programming. I previously worked on health and public services projects where I supported and maintained software for a client. My responsibilities included meeting with our client daily, taking their issues, and working with my team to provide solutions for issues.

I have experience working with Java, web languages, and SQL databases. Recently I have enjoyed learning to use MongoDB. I am an infinitely curious individual and love venturing to the unknown.
